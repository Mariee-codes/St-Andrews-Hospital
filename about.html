Welcome to St Andrews Hospital

St Andrews Hospital is a premier healthcare institution committed to providing exceptional patient care. With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of highly skilled medical professionals, we offer a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the diverse needs of our patients.

Founded in 1985, St Andrews Hospital has grown from a small community clinic to a comprehensive medical center serving thousands of patients annually. Our commitment to excellence in healthcare delivery, education, and research has made us one of the most trusted healthcare providers in the region.

Our History

Since our founding, St Andrews Hospital has evolved from a small community clinic to a comprehensive medical center. Our journey is marked by significant milestones and a constant commitment to improving healthcare standards.

1985

Foundation

St Andrews Hospital was established as a small community clinic with just 15 beds and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals.

1995

Expansion

Expanded to a 100-bed facility with the addition of specialized departments for cardiology, neurology, and pediatrics.

2005

Modernization

Underwent major renovations and technological upgrades, introducing state-of-the-art medical equipment and digital health records.

2015

New Campus

Relocated to our current modern campus, featuring enhanced facilities, expanded services, and improved patient amenities.

Today

Excellence in Healthcare

Now a comprehensive medical center with 250 beds, cutting-edge technology, and a team of over 500 healthcare professionals serving thousands of patients annually.
